About Me

Hi! I'm Tatu Changaya, a crochet lover based in Kenya. My journey began as a childhood hobby and has blossomed into a passion for making beautiful, practical, and fun crochet creations for all ages.

My Story

I started crocheting with my mother, learning the basics and developing my own style over the years. Today, I specialize in modern crochet bags, home décor, and wearable art like hats and scarves.

Why Choose Handmade?

  • Each item is unique—no two pieces are ever exactly alike.
  • Custom orders available for special occasions.
  • Support local artisans and eco-friendly practices.

My Mission

To spread joy and comfort through creative crochet. Whether you’re searching for a special gift, a cozy accessory, or a custom project, I’m here to help!
